# Energy / Labor / Money — the bounding-simplex hypothesis (research seed)

**Status: NAMED, DEFERRED — owner ruling 2026-07-14.** This is a direction note, not a design.
Nothing in this file is implemented; do not cite it as a feature.

## The ruling (condensed from the owner, 2026-07-14)

In addition to the prices/value contradiction (already tracked as the MELT/TSSI value-money
unification program), the ternary relationship of **Money / Labor / Energy** may be key to
Babylon's engine — "almost like it's a kind of bounding simplex." This requires intensive
research before any design work; the owner explicitly deferred it but wanted it named
immediately as a direction the engine needs to go.

## What already exists in the engine (the material anchors — Aleksandrov Test)

Any eventual formalization must trace to these, not float above them:

- **M↔L edge:** MELT — the Monetary Expression of Labor Time — is the subject of the pending
  value-money unification program (`project/research/melt-tvt/`, Path-Forward item B6): one
  per-tick value↔money coefficient, TSSI temporal determination, Marx's two aggregate equalities
  (Σprice = Σvalue, Σprofit = Σsurplus) as Conservation-sentinel invariants. The simplex, if it
  survives research, is most naturally an *extension* of that program with E as the third vertex.
- **E vertex:** `MetabolismSystem` computes the metabolic rift `ΔB = R − (E·η)` and overshoot
  `O = C/B` every tick; Hickel-ERDI ecological-rift data pairs with Φ (currently frozen at 2017).
  Energy is already a first-class per-tick quantity — it is just not yet related to value or money
  by any law.
- **L vertex:** the labor-value substrate via the tensor/Leontief stack (γ visibility, Departments
  I/II/III, per-county Φ, socially-necessary labor time).

## Research directions to evaluate (anchors, not commitments)

- **Podolinsky** (1880s energy-labor accounting; the Marx–Engels correspondence rejecting it) —
  the historical root of the E↔L edge and the classic statement of why naive energy-value
  reduction fails. Any design must answer Engels' objection, not ignore it.
- **Georgescu-Roegen** (entropy economics), **Ayres–Warr** (useful-work growth accounting),
  **Odum** (emergy) — candidate formalizations of energy's role in value production.
- **MuSIASEM** (Giampietro et al.) — multi-scale societal-metabolism accounting; methodologically
  the closest existing framework to a per-territory E/L/M decomposition Babylon could compute.
- **Compositional-data geometry (Aitchison)** — the natural mathematics of a bounded
  3-composition: a state is a point on the 2-simplex, dynamics are flows on it. This is what
  would give "bounding simplex" a precise, testable meaning rather than a metaphor.
- **Fix / Nitzan–Bichler** (capital-as-power contra energy theories of value) — adversarial
  literature; useful as refutation tests for whatever the design claims.

## Constitutional constraints on any eventual design

Every formal construct must pass the Aleksandrov Test (trace to a material relation); no
ungrounded tensors; a new primitive requires a constitutional amendment. If the simplex implies a
conservation law, that law belongs in the Conservation sentinel as a declared invariant — the
same landing pattern as the Marx aggregate equalities in the MELT program.
