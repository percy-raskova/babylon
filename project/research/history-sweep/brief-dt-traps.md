# Design Brief: Doctrine Tree Trap Endings — Historical Grounding

**Scope:** Liquidationism (Reformist), Adventurism (Insurrectionist), Dissociation (Autonomist), Bureaucratic Degeneration (Scientific) — trigger conditions, costs, escape paths.

**Source note:** Citations under `marxists.org/...` and `prolewiki/Exports/Library/...` are primary documents (party minutes, congress transcripts, leader speeches/letters, contemporaneous press). Citations under `prolewiki/Exports/Main/...` are encyclopedia summaries (tertiary), flagged as such and used only for scale-anchoring or naming.

---

## 1. Historical Evidence

### 1.1 Liquidationism (Reformist trunk)

The canonical case is the CPUSA's self-dissolution into the Communist Political Association, **May 20, 1944**. The convention minutes record a unanimous vote — "Motion to dissolve the Communist Party. Carried unanimously" — by 220 regular and 173 alternate delegates from 44 states, a party 25 years old (`marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1944/05/0520-cpusa-convminutes.pdf`). Every state and district convention had *already* endorsed dissolution before the national vote — this was consensus-driven, not a coup. The successor charter substitutes national lineage for class-vanguard language outright: "a non-party organization of Americans which... carries forward the traditions of Washington, Jefferson, Paine, Jackson, and Lincoln" (`.../1944/05/0522-cpa-constitution.pdf`).

Lenin named both the mechanism and the psychology decades earlier: liquidationism is a *rationalization* ("you cannot liquidate what... no longer exists as an organised whole") rooted in cadre "weariness," with betrayal only a downstream label applied after prolonged inaction (`archive/lenin/works/1911/twwliqus/iii.htm`). The 1912 Prague Conference gave the checkable trigger: isolating one partial, legal demand ("the fight for right") from "the sum total of all the revolutionary demands" (`archive/lenin/works/1912/6thconf/pc.htm`).

Correction was staged and slow: Duclos's April 1945 critique produced Browder's suspension "a few days" later, National Committee ratification about a month after (June 18–20), and a full reconstituting congress two months after that (July 26–28) — roughly 14 months from dissolution to correction (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_History of the Communist Party of the United States.txt`). Browder, once ousted, diagnosed the *correcting* faction's new line as a different deviation ("anarchosyndicalism") — overshoot past center, not clean recovery (`cpusa/1946/0201-browder-toyonkersclubcpusa.pdf`).

The slow variant is documented too: CPUSA's deep UAW integration after the 1937 Flint victory shows access/prestige, not weakness, breeding abandonment of independent structure over roughly a decade, ending in expulsion from a dozen CIO unions by 1946 (`history/erol/ncm-7/lrs-uaw.htm`). And a complete lifecycle with no self-repair: the 1975-founded "CPUSA/ML" declared itself the vanguard party Dec. 1978, admitted failure Jan. 1981, and dissolved via Second Congress mid-1983, members "left to float as individuals" — an 8-year arc (`history/erol/ncm-7/mlp-cpusaml.htm`).

### 1.2 Adventurism (Insurrectionist trunk)

The clearest quantified case: Wang Ming's "Left" line and the Fifth Encirclement Campaign, 1933–34. "The Red Army of 300,000 men was reduced to about 30,000 and the Communist Party of 300,000 members to about 40,000" — ~90% and ~87% attrition (`subject/china/documents/cpc/history/01.htm`; corroborated in `reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-1/mswv1_12.htm`). The error incubated **four years** (1931–1935) before the Tsunyi Conference — an emergency, not scheduled, Politburo meeting — corrected it. The proximate trigger was nameable ("military equalitarianism... reached its extreme point... in 1934"), and the episode passed through three sub-stages (adventurism → conservatism → "flightism") over its run.

Lenin's operational definition of adventurism is **mass-rootlessness**, not violence: "tiny groups... with no roots whatever among the masses (and politics without the masses are adventurist politics)" (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Vladimir Lenin_Adventurism.txt`). The same document's positive contrast: the correct-line "Pravdist" current consolidated 4/5 of Russia's class-conscious workers (5,300 of 6,700 groups) over 2.5 years.

The KPD's March Action (March 1921) is the clean single-episode case: an ultra-left uprising against low mass receptivity killed 34 workers and saw 1,500 arrests at Leuna alone, with membership declining then recovering to 200,000+ within a year (`marxists.org/subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/ch15.htm`). The Third Congress (July 1921) retroactively judged it: "The 'theory of the revolutionary offensive' has been registered among the infantile disorders" (`dauve-authier/03.htm`) — trap classification can be a delayed institutional verdict. The scar persisted: the same party hesitated during the genuinely revolutionary 1923 Ruhr crisis, "having 'burnt its fingers' in March 1921" (`subject/stalinism/origins-future/ch1-1a.htm`) — a trap producing an opposite-direction failure ~2.5 years later.

Marighella's "Seven Sins of the Urban Guerrilla" (inexperience, boasting, vanity, overestimating strength, rash action, attacking when the enemy is angriest, failure to plan) is a ready-made trigger checklist (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_Minimanual of the Urban Guerrilla.txt`). The 1979 Greensboro massacre — maximalist slogans with zero readiness for the resulting ambush — is the fatal real instance (`history/erol/ncm-7/basoc-20-years.htm`). Mobilization-forecast collapse gives a discount ratio: the RGO's Jan. 1931 Ruhr strike call claimed 180,000 miners, delivered 65,000 (36%); the RCP's 1980 May Day predicted 50,000, delivered ~600 (1.2%) (`marxists.org/subject/fascism/blick/ch21.htm`; `history/erol/ncm-5/lrs-rcp-crazy.htm`).

### 1.3 Dissociation (Autonomist trunk)

Kronstadt, March 1921, is canonical: a base that had built genuine council organs broke entirely from the vanguard party while still claiming socialist legitimacy — "Soviets without Communists," not counter-revolution in its own telling (`marxists.org/history/ussr/events/kronstadt/izvestia/01.htm`). The same crisis produced, the same day (March 16, 1921), the 10th Congress's Control Commission *and* the "Resolution on the Syndicalist and Anarchist Deviation," which converted "propagation of these ideas" into automatic grounds for expulsion (`history/ussr/government/party-congress/10th/16b.htm`). Its theoretical core is a CLASS_ANALYSIS failure: the "producer" category "lumps together the proletarian with the semi-proletarian and with the small commodity producer" (same source) — a checkable rule: trap fires when class granularity collapses while producerist/guild framing rises.

The AAUD (German factory-council union) outscaled its vanguard-party rival — 150,000 members at its winter 1920–21 peak vs. the KAPD's 40,000 — and still collapsed to near-zero within 2–3 years (`marxists.org/subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/04.htm`). Its program is the clean doctrine-node text: "The councils can be recalled at any time... There is no place for so-called leaders except as advisors." Rühle's speech against Bolshevik centralism (1920) states the trunk's founding axiom: "Centralism is the principle of organization proper to the capitalist-bourgeois era" (`dauve-authier/ch16.htm`) — the Autonomist trunk predicts Bureaucratic Degeneration as an *inevitable* consequence of centralism.

A working counter-mechanic against co-optation: the IWW's term limits — one year, plus six months back "at the point of production" before re-eligibility (`marxists.org/history/usa/unions/iww/1921/kennedy_how.htm`). The Bund's 1903 walkout over federation (Paragraph 2) is the sharp binary-vote version of the same fork: one vote cost the party an entire national delegation (`archive/lenin/works/1904/onestep/c.htm`).

### 1.4 Bureaucratic Degeneration (Scientific trunk)

Gramsci's diagnosis is the direct theoretical source: a party bureaucracy that "organizes itself as a body unto itself, solidary and independent" makes "the party end up anachronistic" (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_Prison Notebooks In Original Italian_Notebook 7.txt`, Q7§77). Trotsky's 1923 five-factor diagnosis names the preconditions: "the international isolation, the cultural backwardness of the masses, their war-weariness and the low level of the economy... the absorption of the Communist cadre in state administration and their habit of command" (`subject/stalinism/origins-future/ch1-1a.htm`). The material floor: Soviet output at ~20% of pre-war level by 1921, plus 12 million typhus deaths 1918–19 — the trap's trigger was material collapse, not villain competence ("it is a great irony of history that precisely the dull-wittedness of the Stalin faction... was the essential secret of their success," same source).

The Lenin Levy (Feb–May 1924) is a concrete dilution event: 250,000 new members admitted to outvote the Left Opposition, cutting the pre-1917 veteran share of the 1924 party to 1% (same source). The 1923 "New Course" resolution (workers' democracy, more frequent congresses, control commissions) carried unanimously and was never enforced.

The purge turning on its own cadre: József Pogány ("John Pepper"), central to 1920s CPUSA factional fights, was arrested July 29, 1937 and shot Feb. 8, 1938 (~194 days) on a fabricated "Hungarian spy" charge, rehabilitated 1956 (~18y10m later) (`marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1938/02/0208-pepper-biography.pdf`). Juliet Stuart Poyntz's ideological break preceded her disappearance by roughly one month (`cpusa/1938/0702-solow-juliastuartpoyntz.pdf`).

The Cultural Revolution gives the fullest arc: the CCP's own post-mortem bounds the trap May 1966–Oct. 1976 (10.5 years), plus ~2 years to the correcting Third Plenary Session (Dec. 1978) and ~2.5 more to the full doctrinal Resolution (June 1981) — ~15 years total, at a 39-million-member party (`subject/china/documents/cpc/history/02.htm`, `.../01.htm`). One year after the trigger the movement's own press was still calling to *escalate* (`subject/china/peking-review/1967/PR1967-01i.htm`).

Comparable US New Communist Movement cases give purge-fraction data: the PRRWO/RWL "Revolutionary Wing" collapse (1976) purged roughly two-thirds of its membership on a fabricated infiltration accusation (`history/erol/ncm-3/wvo-dying.htm`); the OCIC ran three sequential purge campaigns (1979–80: anti-federationism → anti-white-chauvinism → anti-petty-bourgeois-intellectualism), ending with a third-plus expelled and a rump sect of 200–250 (`history/erol/ncm-7/mrl-obit.htm`). The institutional counter is concretely specified: the 10th Congress's Control Commissions — 7 full + 3 candidate centrally, 3–5/2–3 at oblast level, staffed full-time, seniority-gated (10/5/~4 years) (`history/ussr/government/party-congress/10th/16c.htm`), whose mandate explicitly includes counter-disinformation duties.

### 1.5 PatSoc/Strasserism: a trap without escape

Multiple cases converge on **near-zero theoretical-labor cost** for this drift. Hamburg "National Bolshevism" (1919–21) shows the path as a smooth rhetorical bridge: "The councils system groups together all the workers... behind the class interests which are the interests of socialism and the nation" (`marxists.org/subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/appendix3.htm`) — councils framed as vehicles of class *and* nation at once. Wolffheim died in a concentration camp: no safe harbor even in fascist terms. Otto Strasser's 1930 break with Hitler and the Stennes SA revolt (1931) show the mirror case, purged within about a week once it became open revolt, Gregor Strasser's removal made a condition of big-capital patronage (`marxists.org/subject/fascism/blick/ch20.htm`). Spengler's "Prussian Socialism" ("Not Marx's theory, but Frederick William's Prussian practice... will yet displace him") is the literal terminal-node content — hierarchy/duty/blood displacing class analysis while keeping "socialism" vocabulary — and the same source notes this attractor recurred from Luther (1530) to Feder (1920), ~390 years, with no new theory required each time (`.../subject/fascism/blick/ch08.htm`).

## 2. Design Recommendations

Assume 1 game tick = 1 week for conversions. Where history gave no clean number, flagged **NEEDS PLAYTEST**.

### 2.1 Trap attrition / severity

- **`ADVENTURISM_ATTRITION_CEILING = 0.90`** — Wang Ming: Red Army 300k→30k = 90% (`mswv1_12.htm`, `history/01.htm`). Ceiling fraction of cadre+army a fully-triggered Adventurism trap can destroy; scale down for lower-stakes cases.
- **`LIQUIDATIONISM_ATTRITION_CEILING = 0.83`** — 1927 Right-capitulationist collapse: 60,000→10,000 members (`subject/china/documents/cpc/history/01.htm`).
- **`BUREAUCRATIC_PURGE_FRACTION` range 0.33–0.67** — OCIC (⅓+) to PRRWO/RWL (⅔) (`mrl-obit.htm`; `wvo-dying.htm`). Roll within this range rather than fixing a constant; both are real outcomes of the same category.

### 2.2 Trap duration / latency

- **`TRAP_MIN_ESCALATION_TICKS = 52`** (1 year) — CR press was still escalating a full year post-trigger (`PR1967-01i.htm`). Hard floor before any trap becomes escapable.
- **`TRAP_TO_CONGRESS_CORRECTION_TICKS ≈ 208`** (4 years) — Wang Ming, 1931 error to Jan. 1935 Tsunyi correction (`mswv1_12.htm`). Default incubation window before an emergency Congress becomes newly available.
- **`FULL_TRAP_RESOLUTION_TICKS ≈ 780`** (15 years) — Cultural Revolution: 10y to power-break + 2y to correcting Plenum + 2.5y to full doctrinal resolution (`history/02.htm`). Outer bound (ceiling, not target) for how long Bureaucratic Degeneration can persist uncorrected before the engine forces a resolution.
- **`DEFECTION_TO_LIQUIDATION_TICKS ≈ 4`** (1 month) — Poyntz's break to disappearance (`0702-solow-juliastuartpoyntz.pdf`). Single-cadre "vanishes" event, distinct from the org-level trap.
- **`YOUNG_ORG_FRAGILITY_WINDOW_TICKS ≈ 39`** (9 months) — CPUSA/ML split engineered 9 months after Dec. 1978 founding (`history/erol/ncm-7/illegal-party.htm`). Gate a "founding volatility" multiplier on trap probability for newly-founded orgs for their first ~39 ticks.

### 2.3 Congress / rectification cadence

- **`CONGRESS_BASE_CADENCE_TICKS = 104`** (2 years) — RSDLP Party Rules' own minimum (`archive/lenin/works/1904/council/6viii.htm`).
- **`CONGRESS_REPRESSION_SLIPPAGE = 1.5x`** — actual 1908→1912 gap was ~37 months vs. the 2-year rule, ~50% slippage under sustained repression (`archive/lenin/works/1912/6thconf/2.htm`). Scale the base cadence by up to 1.5x under high Repression.
- **`CONGRESS_MAJOR_CADENCE_TICKS ≈ 260–676`** — full trunk-reset congresses are rarer: CPC constitutional cadence 5 years (260 ticks, `Constitution of the Communist Party of China (2002).txt`); actual CPC 7th→8th gap 11 years (572 ticks), 8th→9th gap 13 years (676 ticks) under crisis. Two-tier design recommended: cheap/frequent lower-tier conferences (self_criticism, minor tag adjustment) at ~`CONGRESS_BASE_CADENCE_TICKS`; a rare, expensive full trunk_shift Congress gated at 5+ years, extendable to 10+ under crisis.
- **`RECTIFICATION_BASE_SUCCESS_RATE = 0.02`** — 1904 RSDLP Council: "as many as fifty mediators" attempted reconciliation over ~5 months, one partial success (`archive/lenin/works/1904/council/5ii.htm`). Floor rate for an *undirected* self_criticism action (no structural fix attached); pairing with a genuine structural change should roll well above this.
- **`SELF_CRITICISM_PASS_THRESHOLD = 0.70`** — Mao's own convention: "do the achievements amount to 30 per cent or to 70 per cent of the whole?" (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-4/mswv4_59.htm`). Require an "achievement fraction" ≥0.70 for self_criticism to fully discharge accumulated trap-risk, rather than an automatic reset.
- **`CONTROL_COMMISSION_SIZE = (7, 3)`** full/candidate top tier, (3–5, 2–3) regional, seniority gates 10/5/~4 years (`history/ussr/government/party-congress/10th/16c.htm`). Literal headcount/eligibility template for a standing anti-Bureaucratic-Degeneration institution, purchased once at Theoretical Labor cost.

### 2.4 Trigger formulas

- **Liquidationism:** fire when a doctrine node's action set narrows to a single legal/partial demand (Prague test, `6thconf/pc.htm`) **and** CLASS_ANALYSIS has been monotonically declining for ≥`TRAP_MIN_ESCALATION_TICKS`. Require sustained decline, not one low reading — matches Lenin's "weariness" framing.
- **Adventurism:** fire when a militancy tag crosses a high threshold **while** paired Mass Receptivity (M_r, Epistemic Horizon mechanic) is below Desert (M_r < 0.2). Operationalizes Lenin's "no roots among the masses" test directly and couples the two mechanics.
- **Dissociation:** fire when a federation/autonomy vote (Bund Paragraph 2 template) falls below a set margin AND producerist/guild tag rises while class-differentiation granularity collapses (10th Congress "producer" test). Include a non-terminal outcome — "principled minority holdout," modeled on the Lovestoneite ICO's stable independent trajectory (`fascism.../lovestone/1937/0220-wa-moscowtrials.pdf`) — not every dissociation should cascade to collapse.
- **Bureaucratic Degeneration:** weighted sum of (a) rising cadre-in-administration fraction, (b) falling external-solidarity flow, (c) material scarcity, per Trotsky's five factors. Do NOT gate on a leadership-competence stat — history argues incompetence, not skill, let the trap close.
- **PatSoc pipeline:** zero/near-zero Theoretical Labor cost, confirmed by convergent cases (§1.5). Design law, not a coefficient: the pipeline should progress by default absent counter-investment, mirroring the Economism finding that absent active TL investment a movement does not stay neutral.

### 2.5 Escape / correction design

- Congress outcomes need **three tiers**, not binary: (1) cosmetic/captured — fires, changes nothing (11th CPC Congress, 1977, `history/01.htm`); (2) partial/contested — censure-but-retain (UCP's 1921 24-2-4-10 no-confidence roll call, `0102-ucp-conventionminutes.pdf`); (3) full trunk_shift (rare, ~260+ ticks).
- Rectification that only expels (never reintegrates) carries escalating future-instability cost, per the Lovestoneite critique of CPSU's failure to reabsorb opposition (`0220-wa-moscowtrials.pdf`). Model distinct "reintegration bonus" vs. "expel-only penalty" outcomes of self_criticism.
- **NEEDS PLAYTEST:** exact tag-vector thresholds (what CLASS_ANALYSIS score = "collapsed") have no historical derivation — sources give shapes/orderings, not calibrated cutoffs. Seed at round values (0.2/0.5/0.8, mirroring EH's M_r bands) and tune via playtest.

## 3. Tensions and Open Questions

1. **"No optimal path" is asserted by the tradition itself.** Stalin/Zinoviev/Kamenev's "Leninism or Trotskyism?" (1925) concedes a rival line's correctness is contingent on material conditions, not universal (`anti-trotsky/Leninism or Trotskyism.pdf`); BASOC (1980) states plainly that Right and "Left" errors fail via different mechanisms with neither dominant (`ncm-7/basoc-20-years.htm`). Strong support for the design law — but the engine cannot present any trunk, including a "balanced center," as safe-default without contradicting its own sources.

2. **Congress correction can fail or backfire.** The Third Congress (1921) resolved nothing after the March Action (`dauve-authier/ch16.htm`); the 11th CPC Congress (1977) reaffirmed Cultural Revolution errors instead of fixing them (`history/01.htm`); the Tenth ECCI Plenum (1929) hardened the sectarian line that blinded the KPD to the NSDAP (`fascism/blick/ch18.htm`). The Party Congress mechanic needs a real failure/backfire branch, not a guaranteed corrective.

3. **Self-criticism has genuine and weaponized modes, indistinguishable from outside.** OCIC's "racist errors" self-criticism (`oc-west-maj-present.htm`) was a consolidation purge; East Wind/Seize the Time's merger into LRS (1979) used the identical mechanic benignly (`lrs-east-wind.htm`). Recommend a hidden "captured vs. genuine" flag the player cannot directly observe, mirroring EH's own epistemic-limits thesis.

4. **Infiltration-panic and real infiltration are mechanically indistinguishable from inside.** Poland's CPP self-dissolved in 1938 on an unproven infiltration accusation (30 of 37 CC members shot/imprisoned, `history/erol/ncm-6/costello-poland.htm`); PRRWO's 1975-76 purge cited the same logic (`history/erol/ncm-3/ex-prrwo.htm`). A false-positive infiltration belief can be as lethal as real infiltration — argues for coupling Bureaucratic Degeneration directly to EH's Desert state, but leaves open whether the player should ever be told definitively whether a purge was real, as opposed to that ambiguity persisting even in the post-game record.

5. **Congress cadence figures don't reconcile into one constant.** RSDLP's rule is 2 years; CPC's constitution says 5; observed gaps range ~1 year (crisis congresses like Tsunyi) to 13 years (7th→8th CPC under disruption). Not an error to resolve — the two-tier cadence in §2.3 is designed specifically to avoid collapsing this real range into a false single number.

6. **PatSoc's "zero cost" is well-evidenced for entry, not for exit.** Every §1.5 case shows smooth, cheap entry; escape/reversal evidence is thin (Wolffheim died in a camp; the Traditionalist Worker Party simply dissolved, `prolewiki/Exports/Main/Traditionalist Worker Party.txt`, encyclopedia-tier). Whether the terminal Strasserism node should be genuinely irreversible or merely very costly is not settled by this corpus — needs further research or explicit design ruling.

7. **Theoretical Labor's "coherence" term has qualitative grounding but no numeric derivation.** The "Youth Needs Experience" finding supports practice-time (not just study-hours) as an input (`mswv6_34.htm`), and dilution events (Lenin Levy) and opportunity-cost draws (Lenin's 9-month MEC writing sprint) anchor the *inputs'* behavior — but the multiplicative formula `TL = cadre × study_allocation × coherence` itself is a playtest target, not a historically-derived equation.
