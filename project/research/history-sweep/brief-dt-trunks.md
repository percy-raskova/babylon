# Design Brief: dt-trunks
## Trunk line-struggle — Reformist / Insurrectionist / Autonomist / Scientific splits, two-line struggle, Party Congress + rectification as correction mechanics

**Source note on evidence tiers.** Three tiers of source appear below. (1) *Primary doctrinal/organizational texts* — marxists.org holdings of Lenin, Mao, Stalin, party constitutions, convention minutes, contemporaneous pamphlets. (2) *Near-primary participant retrospectives* — the `history/erol/ncm-*` corpus, written by participants in the 1970s-80s U.S. New Communist Movement about their own organizations, often within years of the events. (3) *Encyclopedia/wiki summaries* — the `prolewiki/Exports` corpus, which is tertiary and sometimes describes very recent (2022) internet-native currents (MAGA Communism, Infrared/InfraWiki); treated as lower-confidence and flagged inline. All file paths are local paths into the historical corpus as supplied.

## 1. Historical evidence

### 1.1 "No optimal path" is not a design metaphor — it recurs as lived history

The clearest finding across the whole corpus is that **the same material conditions produce irreconcilable, defensible readings**, and no faction — including ones later vindicated — could reliably see the fork coming. Days before the 1903 RSDLP split, Lenin told the Congress "the surmise that a rupture may take place over the programme is conjectural in the highest degree. No such tendencies have been discernible" (`archive/lenin/works/1903/2ndcong/9.htm`) — the actual rupture came from an unforeseen axis (Paragraph 1, party-membership definition), confirmed retrospectively (`archive/lenin/works/1904/onestep/k.htm`). Stalin's own "Leninism or Trotskyism?" concedes Trotsky's line "would have been right as opposed to Leninism" under different class composition (`/media/user/data/old-hdd/old-hdd/www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/anti-trotsky/Leninism or Trotskyism.pdf`) — the CPUSA's own canonical anti-Trotsky text grounds trunk-correctness in material conditions, not universal truth. Mao names the law directly: "it is a 'Left' deviation if your judgment is premature, and it is a Right deviation if your judgment lags behind" — the *identical action* is trap or virtue, keyed only to timing (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-5/mswv5_46.htm`). BASOC's 1980 retrospective states it outright: "Right errors slow the potential development of the revolutionary movement, while 'left' errors alienate the masses" (`history/erol/ncm-7/basoc-20-years.htm`); the RCP's own 1978 self-summation adds the coupling term — correcting isolation (a left trap) increases exposure to co-optation (a right trap), and vice versa (`history/erol/ncm-3/rcp-history.htm`) — vulnerability is structurally coupled to whichever trap you most recently escaped, not eliminable by skill.

### 1.2 The four trunks, grounded

**Reformist.** The SPD's 1891 Erfurt Program is the archetype: maximalist revolutionary rhetoric ("the abolition of class rule and of classes themselves") wrapped around a purely parliamentary demands list — suffrage, 8-hour day, tax reform — with no seizure-of-power content (`history/international/social-democracy/1891/erfurt-program.htm`). By 1912 this trunk had built a theoretical-labor economy at scale: 1,000,000 members, 15,000 full-time party workers, 90 daily papers, 62 printing offices (`subject/germany-1918-23/sewell/chapter1.htm`) — then collapsed into social-chauvinism in August 1914, the SPD Reichstag delegation voting unanimously (110-0) for war credits (same source). Lenin's economism debate (`archive/lenin/works/1901/witbd/ch02.htm`, `ch03.htm`) states this trunk's founding axiom: consciousness arises spontaneously from the economic struggle, no theoretical labor required — which Lenin brands "the ideological enslavement of the workers by the bourgeoisie." Foster's 1921 "boring-from-within" thesis is the American manifesto against dual unionism, warning purity-seeking splits bleed off "not over 1 percent of the whole membership" — exactly the militant fraction mass organizations need (`history/usa/unions/tuel/1921/0528-foster-wherefore.pdf`).

**Insurrectionist.** Lenin's 1912 formula — "To build the party means strengthening and increasing the number of illegal nuclei, surrounding them by a network of legal strong-points" (cited in `history/erol/ncm-7/illegal-party.htm`) — is the trunk's core architecture: hidden core, permeable mass-facing surface. The United Communist Party's October 1920 "Boycott the Election!" proclamation is a clean, dated specimen: explicit rejection of electoral participation, explicit call to convert a general strike into armed uprising (`history/usa/parties/cpusa/1920/1000-ucp-boycotttheelection.pdf`). Lenin's own definition of the trunk's shadow — *adventurism* — is precise: "an internally contradictory, unprincipled, unstable trend which conceals emptiness behind high-sounding promises" (`archive/lenin/works/1902/dec/00.htm`), and later, more mechanically: tiny groups "with no roots whatever among the masses (and politics without the masses are adventurist politics)" (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Vladimir Lenin_Adventurism.txt`).

**Autonomist.** Council communism's stated program rejects the vanguard-party mechanism outright for workers'-council self-organization (`prolewiki/Exports/Main/Council communism.txt`); the AAUD's founding program (Leipzig, Dec 1920) states the doctrine baldly: "councils can be recalled at any time... There is no place for so-called leaders except as advisors" (`subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/04.htm`). Lenin's fight against Bund federalism at the 2nd RSDLP Congress (1903) is the direct fork against this trunk from the Scientific side: "Federation is harmful because it sanctions segregation and alienation, elevates them to a principle, to a law" (`archive/lenin/works/1903/2ndcong/13.htm`) — but Lenin also distinguishes *safe* autonomy (operational independence, retained duty of obedience) from the *trapped* version (full federation, no accountability) in the same speech, a two-tier design already present in the source. Kronstadt, March 1921, is the trunk's canonical Dissociation trap: a base that built genuine council organs, then broke entirely from the vanguard party while still claiming socialist legitimacy — demanding new soviet elections, not counter-revolution (`history/ussr/events/kronstadt/izvestia/01.htm`, `izvestia/11.htm`).

**Scientific.** Kautsky's line, quoted approvingly by Lenin, is the trunk's founding axiom: "socialist consciousness is something introduced into the proletarian class struggle from without" (`archive/lenin/works/1901/witbd/ch02.htm`) — doctrine is a purchased input, not a spontaneous byproduct, the direct textual justification for Theoretical Labor as a real, non-optional resource draw. The trunk's own named trap, **Bureaucratic Degeneration**, has an explicit structural (not moral) diagnosis from Trotsky, reproduced in `subject/stalinism/origins-future/ch1-1a.htm` and `ch1-2a.htm`: international isolation, cultural/economic backwardness, war-weariness, and "the absorption of the Communist cadre in state administration and their habit of command." Gramsci's Prison Notebooks state the mechanism identically: a party bureaucracy that "organizes itself as a body unto itself, solidary and independent" causes the party to become anachronistic (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_Prison Notebooks In Original Italian_Notebook 7.txt`).

### 1.4 Party Congress and rectification as correction mechanics

Congresses recur at real, citable cadences: RSDLP Party Rules mandated "not less than once in two years" (`archive/lenin/works/1904/council/6viii.htm`), slipping in practice to ~3.1 years (Dec 1908–Jan 1912) under sustained repression, ~55% cadence slippage (`archive/lenin/works/1912/6thconf/2.htm`). The CPC's 2002 Constitution codifies a 5-year cadence (Article 18/21), turnover capped at "not exceed one-fifth" per cycle (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_Constitution of the Communist Party of China (2002).txt`); actual CPC Congress-to-Congress gaps ran 11 years (7th→8th) and 13 years (8th→9th, Cultural-Revolution disrupted) (`subject/china/documents/cpc/8th_cong_opening.htm`). The DPRK's Workers' Party averaged one Congress every 5.8 years across its first 35 years, each preceded by about a year of preparation (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_Kim Jong Il_ Brief History.txt`).

Congresses can also be **triggered by deadlock rather than clock**: the RSDLP Council resolved to convene an emergency Third Congress after citing five-plus months of institutional paralysis (`archive/lenin/works/1904/council/3.htm`), though the actual Congress didn't convene until 14 months later. A concrete rectification-body model comes from the 10th RSDLP(b) Congress's Control Commissions (March 1921): 7 full + 3 candidate members centrally, staffed full-time, seniority-gated at 10 years (central), 5 years (oblast), "since Feb 1917" (guberniia) (`history/ussr/government/party-congress/10th/16c.htm`).

Congress outcomes are demonstrably **not guaranteed to correct**: the CCP's 11th Congress (Aug 1977) "reaffirmed the erroneous theories... instead of correcting them," the actual correction arriving only at the Third Plenary Session 16 months later (`subject/china/documents/cpc/history/01.htm`); the 1959 Lushan Plenum's condemnation of Peng Dehuai was later ruled "entirely wrong," a 22-year gap between a wrong verdict and its reversal (same source). Rectification outcomes range from full reintegration ("curing the sickness to save the patient," `reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-3/mswv3_06.htm`) to cosmetic self-criticism theater that changes nothing (`history/erol/ncm-3/boc.htm`), to weaponized loyalty-confession rituals consolidating power rather than correcting doctrine (`history/erol/ncm-6/oc-west-maj-present.htm`). A concrete numeric bar appears in Mao's land-reform convention: work is judged "in the main" approved only above 70% achievement (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-4/mswv4_59.htm`); the RWL's 1976 self-summation documents extracting "the 5% which is correct" even from an otherwise-rejected hostile critique (`history/erol/ncm-1a/rwl-history.htm`).

### 1.5 Theoretical Labor economics

Lenin set an explicit minimum cadence for a party organ to count as a real periodical rather than occasional leaflets: 2-4 issues per month (`archive/lenin/works/1902/feb/01b.htm`). Bolshevik Pravda (1912-13) is cited directly in Comintern theses as the model of a worker-financed party press, "financing it from their own wages," sustained over roughly two years (`history/international/comintern/3rd-congress/party-theses.htm`). Party-founding itself required real cadre aggregation, not a single vanguard decision: ~20 independent study circles merged into the St. Petersburg League of Struggle before any party (`history/erol/ncm-1a/premises.htm`); the "Ten Great Relationships" line was drafted from consultation with "over thirty cadres" (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-8/mswv8_64.htm`). CPUSA's own 1930 tabulated cadre-to-mass leverage gives a concrete conversion figure: ~1 cadre per 10 members in fully controlled organizations vs. ~1 per 175 in merely influenced ones (`history/usa/parties/cpusa/1930/0600-cpusa-languagework.pdf`).

### 1.6 The PatSoc pipeline — real drift chains

Hamburg "National Bolshevism" (Wolffheim/Laufenberg, 1919-21) is the single richest primary case: the drift ran through **shared vocabulary**, not a discrete ideological break — councils are described as vehicles of "class interests which are the interests of socialism and the nation," with "the factory councils will become an element of national unity" (`subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/appendix3.htm`). The pipeline required no new theoretical investment; it recycled existing council-communist language. Support reached "several thousand Hamburg workers" before collapsing by 1920-21; Wolffheim himself later died in a concentration camp — the pipeline offered no safe harbor even on fascist terms (same source). The KPD literally fraternized with Nazis during the 1923 Ruhr crisis, with a Nazi orator advising communists to "rid themselves of the Jews" at a joint meeting (`subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/ch15.htm`); a KPD-linked "Group of German Communist Army Officers" adopted Spenglerian "Prussian socialism" wholesale during the same crisis (same source). Spengler's own doctrine, quoted directly, gives the terminal node's content: "Not Marx's theory, but Frederick William's Prussian practice... trains the individual in his duty to the whole" (`subject/fascism/blick/ch08.htm`) — hierarchy/duty/blood displacing class analysis while retaining "socialism" vocabulary. The Manifesto's own taxonomy of Feudal/Petit-Bourgeois/"German-True" Socialism (same source) is a ready-made node-naming scheme, and the same anti-usury "spare industrial capital, blame finance capital" move recurs from Luther (1530) to Feder (1920) — **390 years of recurrence independent of any specific theoretical investment** (same source), strong evidence the pipeline should cost near-zero Theoretical Labor. Otto Strasser's 1930 break with Hitler and the Stennes SA revolt show the mirror-image case from inside fascism (`subject/fascism/blick/ch20.htm`).

*Lower-confidence, contemporary (encyclopedia-tier):* ProleWiki entries describe a 2022 "MAGA Communism" current explicitly compared to Strasserism by its own proponents, and a documented ~2-month latency from public doctrine-hardening (Aug 2022) to an organizational split founding "InfraWiki" (Oct 2022) (`prolewiki/Exports/Main/MAGACommunism.txt`, `prolewiki/Exports/Main/Infrared.txt`). These are wiki summaries of very recent, small internet-native currents — useful for the pipeline's cadence but not load-bearing in the way the Hamburg case is.

Browder's 1944 dissolution rhetoric is the clean American Liquidationism/PatSoc-adjacent case: "carries forward the traditions of Washington, Jefferson, Paine, Jackson, and Lincoln" replacing class-vanguard language in the very charter of the successor organization (`history/usa/parties/cpusa/1944/05/0522-cpa-constitution.pdf`).

## 2. Design recommendations

**Tick assumption.** The engine's canonical run is described elsewhere as `tick/520`; assuming a 10-year canonical run, this implies **1 tick ≈ 1 week (52 ticks/year)**. All conversions below use this assumption and should be confirmed against the actual `SimulationConfig`/tick semantics before being written into `defines.yaml` — flagged wherever used.

### 2.6 Regional/scenario bias

The corpus shows a **U.S.-specific asymmetry**: "the overriding danger historically within the communist movement has been right opportunism in the form of economism" for "close to fifty years" (1920s-1975) (`history/erol/ncm-3/mloc-central-task.htm`). Recommend a scenario-level weight — in a Core/hegemonic-state scenario, bias trap-trigger probability toward Liquidationism (Reformist) over Adventurism at roughly 2:1 or 3:1, tunable per region rather than global.

## 3. Tensions and open questions

1. **Congress cadence has no single canonical value.** RSDLP rules say 2 years; CPC constitution says 5; WPK actual average is 5.8; the U.S. underground parties in 1920-21 ran closer to annual. There is no historically "correct" default — recommend making cadence itself a doctrine-tree property (a purchasable/tunable node) rather than a single global constant, with 104 ticks (2 yr) as the pre-power default and 260 ticks (5 yr) as the post-power/consolidated-ruling-party default, per §2.1.

2. **The Congress correction mechanism is not reliably corrective.** History shows Congresses that fail to correct (CCP 11th Congress, 1977, ratifying the very error it was meant to fix — `subject/china/documents/cpc/history/01.htm`) and Congress verdicts later reversed after decades (Lushan/Peng Dehuai, wrong in 1959, reversed in 1981 — same source). If the mechanic always resolves a trap on Congress-firing, it contradicts this evidence. Recommend the Congress action have an explicit failure probability, and a separate "verdict confidence" value that can be revised by a *later* Congress rather than being ground truth.

3. **Rectification's default disposition is contested in the record itself.** Mao's own doctrine treats rectification as reintegrative by default ("curing the sickness to save the patient," `reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-3/mswv3_06.htm`), and expulsion/faction-flip as reserved for actual defection. But the OCIC and PRRWO cases show rectification actively weaponized into confession rituals that consolidate power rather than correct doctrine (`history/erol/ncm-6/oc-west-maj-present.htm`, `history/erol/ncm-3/wvo-dying.htm`). The mechanic needs both modes as distinct outcomes of the *same* action type, not a single deterministic rectification result — which mode fires should probably depend on which trunk currently holds Congress-floor control (see the vote-time procedural coup and floor-time-asymmetry findings, `archive/lenin/works/1904/onestep/c.htm`, `history/international/comintern/3rd-congress/organisation/note.htm`).

4. **Desert-state falsification is bidirectional, contradicting the EH brief's State-vs-Revolution framing.** Both the CPA and CLP "freely indulged in 'padding' membership and falsifying records... particularly when reports were made to Moscow" in 1920-21 (`history/usa/parties/cpusa/1921/0713-grimes-boimemo.pdf`) — at the exact moment of the 1919 CP/CLP split, the two successor factions' combined self-reported membership *exceeded* the parent body's true membership (`history/usa/parties/cpusa/1919/09/0902-nycall-communistsplit.pdf`). This means the Desert vision-state (masked data that looks normal) must also apply reflexively to a faction's own Theoretical Labor ledger, not only to enemy surveillance of the masses — an open design question for how EH and DT share state.

5. **The PatSoc pipeline's cost model is under-evidenced at high-confidence timescales.** The strongest primary case (Hamburg, 1919-21) gives a ~1-2 year collapse window; the only citable *fast* latency (≈2 months) comes from a 2022 encyclopedia summary of a fringe internet current, not a primary source. Recommend keeping `PATSOC_DRIFT_TO_SPLIT_LATENCY_TICKS` explicitly marked low-confidence and playtest-calibrated rather than treating it as historically derived with the same confidence as the Hamburg number.

6. **Trap severity bifurcates into two shapes the mechanic may need to distinguish.** Some traps are catastrophic and fast (Adventurism: 83-90% attrition inside a few years, per §2.2), others are silent and slow (the SPD's Reformist infrastructure did not collapse in membership terms before 1914 — it simply decayed ideologically over "decades of comparatively peaceful development" before a sudden, catastrophic *doctrinal* rupture with no matching membership crash, `archive/lenin/works/1917/staterev/preface.htm`). A single `TRAP_ATTRITION_FRACTION` may not fit both shapes — recommend a second axis (doctrinal-rupture-without-attrition) for Liquidationism specifically, distinct from the membership-collapse traps that fit the CCP data cleanly.
