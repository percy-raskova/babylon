# Design Brief: Mass-Line Intelligence — Social Investigation, Base Areas, Intelligence Through Relationships

**Theme:** eh-mass-line · **Mechanic:** Epistemic Horizon (M_r, I_c, Desert/Mud/Water)

---

## 1. Historical Evidence

### 1.1 The core thesis, stated almost verbatim by the sources

The clearest primary-source articulation of "intelligence is not about presence but about relationship" is Mao's direct comparison of information leakage in Red vs. White areas: *"In the White areas the mere leakage of a piece of information may cause defeat in a subsequent engagement, but in the Red areas such leakage is often not a very serious matter"* (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-1/mswv1_12.htm`). Identical information carries opposite strategic weight depending on relationship. Treat this as the mechanic's citation-grade thesis statement.

The CPUSA's 1935 Organizer's Manual makes the same claim about the factory Shop Unit under conditions of terror and illegality: it "will remain the most solid link with the masses under any conditions" (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1935/07/organisers-manual/ch03.htm`) — a workplace cell built on real relationship survives repression that destroys presence-based, public intelligence.

Kautsky's "from without" thesis, quoted approvingly by Lenin, is the theoretical root: *"socialist consciousness is something introduced into the proletarian class struggle from without and not something that arose within it spontaneously"* (`archive/lenin/works/1901/witbd/ch02.htm`). Consciousness/receptivity must be actively carried in by organized relationship-building — never simply observed or awaited. This directly underwrites "you cannot SCOUT your way to intelligence."

### 1.2 The design law has a historical failure case, not just a success case

Lenin's own 1917 writing records the design law's *violation* and its cost: "the inexperienced agitator of the 'good old days' went simply and directly to the countryside — and got a beating for his pains" (`archive/lenin/works/1917/tasks/ch05.htm`). Direct, unearned agitation into an un-primed population actively backfires — grounds for making a SCOUT-style probe a *negative*-EV action against low-M_r territory, not merely a wasted one. The same chapter frames low receptivity as *structurally honest, not stupid*: "The mass believers in revolutionary defencism are honest, not in the personal, but in the class sense" — deceived by pride and apparent regime change, not raw fact suppression (same source). This argues M_r's class-factor term should model sincere false consciousness, not merely ignorance.

### 1.3 The mass-line cycle as the literal mechanism

The Call's 1976 canonical restatement of Mao's method: "take the ideas of the masses (scattered and unsystematic ideas) and concentrate them" then return and test in action — "communists can no more substitute themselves for the actions of the masses than the actions of the masses could by themselves take the place of communist consciousness and leadership" (`history/erol/ncm-3/call-mass-line.htm`). This is the two-way relational loop that should raise M_r/I_c, as against passive propaganda distribution which should not. Mao's own three-tier stratification of any mass population — "the relatively active, the intermediate and the relatively backward" (same source) — is a direct historical precedent for modeling class_factor as a discrete 3-tier population split rather than a single scalar.

The Hunan Peasant Movement Report gives an actual numeric version of this split: poor peasants 70%, middle peasants 20%, landlords/rich peasants 10% of Changsha County's rural population (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-1/mswv1_2.htm`), with poor peasants organizing instantly and leading, middle peasants waiting, and landlords resisting until coerced.

### 1.4 Growth is bursty and threshold-triggered, not smooth

The Hunan peasant-association case is the clearest phase-change data point: after nine months of flat organizing, membership "jumped to two million" (masses under leadership: ten million) once a material precondition (the Northern Expedition removing fear of reprisal) was met — roughly a 5x jump in about four months (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-1/mswv1_2.htm`). The 1955 agricultural-cooperative wave repeats the pattern almost exactly: from 16.9 million to over 50 million additional peasant households in "the space of a few months" (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-5/mswv5_47.htm`) — a second ~4x phase-change data point.

The IWW Agricultural Workers Organization No. 400 shows the same shape from zero: founded April 1915, over 8,000 initiated members by Fall 1916 — roughly 18 months, no repression yet in play (`history/erol/ncm-1/25years.htm`, sourced as `www.marxists.org/history/usa/unions/iww/1930/25years.htm`). Notably, membership "more than doubled" through calendar 1917 *despite* the September 5, 1917 nationwide anti-IWW raids — repression did not crash receptivity instantly; prior relationship-capital bought a lag before organizing was "greatly hampered" in following months (same source).

The February 1917 Petrograd garrison flip — 66,000 troops joining strikers within 24 hours of firing on them (`history/ussr/events/timeline/1917.htm`) — and the Kornilov affair (an elite loyalist corps dissolving from within in 3 days once agitators reached it, same source) establish an upper ceiling for how fast M_r can spike under acute crisis.

### 1.5 Decay is asymmetric: slow to build, fast to collapse

Kronstadt, March 1921, is the richest single decay case in the corpus. The garrison's own resolution states the collapse in explicitly relational terms: "The Communist Party... has become separated from the masses" (`history/ussr/events/kronstadt/izvestia/01.htm`). Within roughly 9-13 days of the uprising's start, more than 130 individually-named party-departure declarations accumulated in a single bulletin issue (`history/ussr/events/kronstadt/izvestia/08.htm`), and by issue #12 the besieged garrison was already documenting military casualties (`history/ussr/events/kronstadt/izvestia/12.htm`) — a floor of roughly two weeks for M_r to invert from institutional loyalty to open rupture under acute material stress.

The SPD's WWI membership collapse is a slower-motion version of the same curve: 1,000,000 members in 1914 down to 200,000 by September 1917 — an 80% defection over about three years of war betrayal (`subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/ch04.htm`), tracking discrete strike-wave escalations (50,000 → 250,000 → 1,000,000+ strikers) rather than a smooth curve.

By contrast, rebuilding is slow. CPUSA's illegal apparatus after the January 1920 Palmer Raids took about two months to reconstitute ("very hard to see people at that time," per M. Golos's 1940 oral history, `www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1940/0000-mgolos-johnsoninterview.pdf`); the party's dues-paying base recovered to a comparable scale (5,801 members) within about 11 months of the arrest of 5,000-6,000 (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1921/0102-ucp-conventionminutes.pdf`); the Bulgarian CP took roughly a decade (1923-1933) to rebuild past pre-suppression strength after the 1923 insurrection's defeat (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_Georgi Dimitrov_ Selected Speeches and Articles.txt`). Print/propaganda infrastructure rebuilds much faster than the underground apparatus itself — the CPA's Russian-language paper was reorganized in two weeks after a rival faction seized it (same Golos interview).

### 1.6 Base areas and liberated zones: a real three-state taxonomy

Mao's wartime writing supplies an almost literal Desert/Mud/Water taxonomy that predates the game design: enemy-held territory, "guerrilla zones" ("held by the guerrillas when they are there and by the puppet regime when they are gone"), and consolidated "base areas," with explicit named transition criteria (enemy destroyed, puppet regime broken, masses aroused, mass organizations formed, political power established) for crossing into the base-area/Water tier (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-2/mswv2_08.htm`). Crucially, the same source states reversal is bidirectional and can cascade two tiers in one move under a single leadership error. Nkrumah's later doctrine for the African liberation wars restates the same three-zone structure explicitly keyed to "the level of the people's awareness and participation" rather than force ratio (`prolewiki/Exports/Library/Library_Handbook of Revolutionary Warfare.txt`).

A concrete survivability floor: the Jinggangshan base held for four months against KMT expeditionary forces 2x-4.5x its size (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-1/mswv1_3.htm`), and Mao names organizational strength as one of five *conjunctive* preconditions for Red political power's persistence, alongside inter-warlord conflict, prior mass mobilization, a live national revolutionary situation, and an adequate regular army (same source) — Water-state persistence should be gated on multiple independent conditions, not cadre count alone.

### 1.7 Falsification is bidirectional, and it "looks normal"

The DoJ's own 1920 instructions to arrange Communist Party meetings *through* undercover informants specifically to enable synchronized raids (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/government/dept-justice/1920/0524-doj-provocateurs.pdf`) shows the state engineering the object it surveils, not merely observing it — the textbook Desert mechanism. But falsification runs the other way too: both the Communist Party of America and Communist Labor Party "freely indulged in 'padding' membership and falsifying records" in reports to Moscow (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1921/0713-grimes-boimemo.pdf`); the KPD's own leadership inflated 1921 March Action strike figures roughly 3-5x over the true count (`subject/germany-1918-23/dauve-authier/ch15.htm`); Castro's Batista-era jailers fabricated internally-coherent, format-correct casualty reports (`www.marxists.org/history/cuba/archive/castro/1953/10/16.htm`). Desert-state design should model *symmetric* self-deception, not a one-directional state weapon.

Zubatovism — Okhrana-sponsored "legal" unions under police tutelage (`archive/lenin/works/1901/witbd/ch02.htm`, `archive/lenin/works/1902/oct/15.htm`) — is the clean historical case of the state manufacturing *counterfeit* Water-tier intimacy: real reforms promised, real hollowness underneath.

### 1.8 Concrete cadre-density and cadence numbers

CPUSA's 1930 Language Bureau report gives real cadre-to-mass leverage ratios: ~1 cadre per 10 masses in fully controlled organizations (5,000/50,000) vs. ~1 per 175 in merely-influenced ones (800/140,000) — and, in the same report, "organizations consisting of over a million members... have no Party members and no influence," a literal numbered Desert zone at scale (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1930/0600-cpusa-languagework.pdf`). The Profintern's 1929 Strasbourg strike doctrine specifies committee-representation density (1:25-50 small shops, 1:100-200 large shops, `www.marxists.org/history/international/profintern/strike_strategy/ch05.htm`); CPUSA's 1935 Organizer's Manual caps Shop Unit size at 10-12 before mandatory splitting (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1935/07/organisers-manual/ch03.htm`). The IWW's job-branch bulletin system ran weekly, aggregated at annual/semi-annual conventions (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/unions/iww/1921/kennedy_how.htm`); the Comintern's Third Congress theses mandate roughly weekly cell reporting (`www.marxists.org/history/international/comintern/3rd-congress/party-theses.htm`).

### 1.9 Theoretical Labor as investment in receptivity, not abstract study

Mao's 1943 "Get Organized!" speech gives an explicit ratio: cadre should devote 90% of effort to mass welfare before the remaining 10% (extraction capacity) becomes viable (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-3/mswv3_17.htm`). The CPUSA Organizer's Manual computes a circulation-to-reach multiplier directly: 200-300 readers per neighborhood paper, aggregated, was calculated to yield close to a million-worker relationship footprint (`www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1935/07/organisers-manual/ch03.htm`). Conversely, Mao repeatedly criticizes over-investment in document study divorced from field contact — 40 days of document study observed against his proposed ~1 day plus a week's discussion before going to the countryside (`reference/archive/mao/selected-works/volume-9/mswv9_38.htm`) — a real opportunity-cost tension for Theoretical Labor spend without cadre-presence time.

## 2. Design Recommendations

Each coefficient below states the historical number(s), the derivation into a weekly game tick (the corpus's own cadences — Comintern cell reporting, IWW bulletins — run roughly weekly, so 1 tick ≈ 1 week is used as the conversion baseline unless noted), and a recommended starting value flagged for playtest calibration where history is silent on magnitude.

**2.1 M_r growth is not linear — model it as regime-switching.**
Historical growth (Hunan 1926-27, agricultural co-ops 1955, IWW AWO 1915-16) is flat for months, then jumps ~4-5x within a single season once a material threshold clears (`mswv1_2.htm`, `mswv5_47.htm`, `25years.htm`). Recommend: M_r accrual per AGITATE_LOCALLY/SOCIAL_INVESTIGATION tick stays near a low baseline (recommend 0.01-0.02/tick, **needs playtest calibration** — no source gives a per-action increment) until a `desperation` or `class_factor` threshold is crossed, at which point apply a one-time multiplier of **4x-5x** to the accumulated rate for a bounded window (recommend 4-6 ticks ≈ month, derived from "space of a few months"/"~4 months" in both Hunan and 1955 sources). This produces the historical burst shape rather than a smooth logistic curve.

**2.2 M_r decay ceiling (collapse rate under acute rupture).** Kronstadt: garrison M_r toward the ruling party went from institutional loyalty to open declared rupture in **9-13 days** (`izvestia/08.htm`, `izvestia/01.htm`, `izvestia/12.htm`). Converting to weekly ticks: **collapse-to-zero in 1-2 ticks** once a Dissociation-class trigger fires (mass betrayal by leadership, e.g. the 1922 Shopmen's Strike collapse on leadership sellout, `unions/tuel/1922/1000-foster-shopmensstrike.pdf`). Recommend this as a hard floor, not a tunable default: **no ordinary per-tick decay should be able to produce a 2-tick full collapse** — only a scripted "leadership betrayal" or "Desert-exposure" event should be permitted to invoke it.

**2.3 M_r decay rate absent renewed investment (slow attrition).** The Polish United Workers' Party's working-class membership share fell 61%→45% over 6 years (1949-1955), ~2.7 points/year (`history/erol/ncm-6/costello-poland.htm`). Converted: **≈0.05%/week** baseline erosion of mass-base composition absent active mass-line reinvestment. Recommend as the passive per-tick M_r decay floor in the *absence* of a rupture event — orders of magnitude slower than the rupture-collapse ceiling in 2.2, confirming the asymmetry (slow build/erode, fast rupture).

**2.4 Underground-apparatus reconstitution after a severe repression shock.** CPUSA rebuilt its illegal apparatus in **~2 months** after the January 1920 Palmer Raids (`0000-mgolos-johnsoninterview.pdf`), while the printed-propaganda channel specifically rebuilt in **~2 weeks** (same source). Recommend two separate recovery-rate constants: `underground_network_recovery_ticks ≈ 8` (2 months), `propaganda_channel_recovery_ticks ≈ 2` (2 weeks) — i.e., after a Desert-triggering rollup, communication capacity (raising public-baseline I_c) recovers **4x faster** than relational/cadre network capacity (raising true M_r).

**2.5 Cadre re-insertion lag after a state Desert-purge.** The DoJ inserted a replacement agent into the Buffalo CP local **20 days** after the January 2, 1920 mass raids (`0524-doj-provocateurs.pdf`). Recommend: state-side informant re-insertion after a successful counter-infiltration sweep should have a cooldown of **~3 ticks (~3 weeks)**, not instant — matching this figure, and consistent with the low-value informant's short (~3 month, `Sept-Nov 1919`) tenure in the same source, which argues single-informant I_c gains should decay/cap quickly rather than compound.

**2.6 Cadre:mass leverage ratio (Intel Confidence scaling).** CPUSA's 1930 tabulated ratios: ~1:10 in fully-controlled organizations (5,000/50,000), ~1:175 in merely-influenced ones (800/140,000) (`0600-cpusa-languagework.pdf`). Recommend I_c's cadre-presence term scale territory coverage using these two ratios as the "controlled node" vs. "influenced node" tiers, with **zero-cadre Desert as the explicit default** for the rest of a territory's population — the same report documents over a million workers with 0 cadre and 0 influence at the same historical moment the Party had real coverage elsewhere. Recommend defaulting new/unclaimed territory nodes to Desert, not Mud, unless a mass-line action has touched them.

**2.7 Cell-size security cap.** CPUSA's 1935 Organizer's Manual: max 10-12 members before mandatory Shop Unit split (`ch03.htm`). Recommend `ESTABLISH_CONTACT_NETWORK` cap contact-network node size at **10-12 cadre** per cell; exceeding it should trigger an automatic split action (cost: 1 tick, no M_r loss) rather than a flat infiltration-risk multiplier climbing unbounded — the historical response was structural (split), not defensive posture.

**2.8 Committee-representation density.** Profintern 1929: 1:25-50 (small shop), 1:100-200 (large shop) (`ch05.htm`). Recommend these as the two default `cadre_density` presets for SOCIAL_INVESTIGATION's territory-coverage roll, small-population nodes using the denser ratio.

**2.9 Mass-line action cadence.** IWW job-branch bulletins ran weekly, aggregated at conventions annually/semi-annually (`kennedy_how.htm`); Comintern Third Congress mandated roughly weekly cell reporting (`party-theses.htm`). Recommend `AGITATE_LOCALLY` and `SOCIAL_INVESTIGATION` both operate on a **1-tick (weekly) cadence** as their natural refresh rate, consistent with the game's existing per-tick structure — this is the strongest direct historical anchor for the tick-length-to-real-time mapping in this mechanic specifically.

**2.10 Theoretical-Labor-to-M_r conversion.** CPUSA's own circulation math: 200-300 readers/paper aggregated to ~1,000,000 mass contacts (`ch03.htm`); Mao's 90%-mass-service / 10%-extraction ratio (`mswv3_17.htm`). Recommend: **AGITATE_LOCALLY's M_r yield should scale with (TL committed to circulation) × a conversion multiplier**, historically anchored around 3,000-5,000x (200-300 readers → ~1,000,000 contacts, i.e., ~1 paper-unit of TL ≈ 3,000-5,000 mass-contact-equivalents) — flagged as an order-of-magnitude anchor only, not a validated per-tick rate; **needs playtest calibration** for the actual M_r delta this should produce.

**2.11 Desert-state falsification is symmetric — implement it on both sides.** The DOJ deliberately staged meetings to enable raids (`0524-doj-provocateurs.pdf`); the CPA/CLP both padded membership reports to Moscow (`0713-grimes-boimemo.pdf`); the KPD overclaimed March Action strikers 3-5x (`ch15.htm`). Recommend a symmetric Desert mechanic: below `M_r < 0.2`, **both** the State's read of the Revolution's territory *and* the Revolution's own self-report to its Theoretical Labor/Doctrine ledger should be subject to a falsification roll — factions can and historically did lie to their own patrons, not only to the enemy.

**2.12 Base-area persistence as a conjunctive gate.** Mao's five preconditions for durable Red political power (`mswv1_3.htm`) argue Water-state persistence (not just M_r crossing 0.8) should require **AND**-gating on: (a) cadre/organization strength, (b) prior mass mobilization in the territory, (c) absence of overwhelming immediate enemy concentration, (d) a viable regular-army/defense capacity. Recommend implementing Water-state as reachable via the M_r formula alone, but *durable* (resistant to a single-tick State action) only when at least 2 of these 4 conditions independently hold — otherwise it should be a fragile "Water spike" rather than a stable base area, matching the historical Jinggangshan four-month hold under 2-4.5x force disadvantage as the durability floor.

**2.13 Action-specific event: the "premature agitator" penalty.** History (`ch05.htm`, Lenin 1917) explicitly names direct agitation against unprimed masses as an active backfire, not a null result. Recommend `AGITATE_LOCALLY` targeted at a Desert-tier (M_r < 0.2) territory carry a **negative M_r delta** (recommend -0.05, **needs playtest calibration**) rather than zero yield — mechanically enforcing "you cannot scout your way to intelligence" as a punished, not merely wasted, action.

**2.14 Informant/infiltration as a discrete catastrophic event, not gradual decay.** Mikhailov's 1895 infiltration of the St. Petersburg League of Struggle rolled up a founding cell in a single night raid (`archive/lenin/works/1901/witbd/ch02.htm`); William O'Neal's embedding inside the Chicago BPP took ~18 months to mature into the intelligence that enabled the fatal December 1969 raid (`prolewiki/.../Library_The Assassination of Fred Hampton.txt`). Recommend infiltration be modeled as a **binary, discrete event** with a build-up latency (recommend 6-18 months / 24-72 ticks depending on target depth, **needs playtest calibration** for exact distribution) that, once triggered, zeroes local M_r/I_c for that node in a single tick — not a continuous erosion.

## 3. Tensions and Open Questions

- **Foco vs. mass-line boundary case.** Castro's 1953 Moncada attack explicitly expected mass support to *follow* a spectacular armed action (the planned broadcast of five revolutionary laws) rather than grow from prior relationship-building (`www.marxists.org/history/cuba/archive/castro/1953/10/16.htm`). This is the historical case that would justify letting *some* dramatic ACTION generate I_c/M_r directly — in tension with the design law that only mass-line actions raise intelligence. Recommend flagging this as the deliberately-excluded exception rather than silently permitting it; if a "propaganda of the deed" action type is ever added, it should be tagged as high-variance/high-failure-rate to reflect that Moncada itself failed militarily.

- **Congress vs. crisis correction.** Party Congresses in this corpus sometimes convene on cadence (Sixth RSDLP(b) Congress, `www.marxists.org/history/ussr/events/timeline/1917.htm`) and sometimes emerge from emergency crisis (the Zunyi Conference, mid-Long-March, `subject/china/documents/cpc/history/01.htm`). The EH brief doesn't need to resolve this (it's DT territory) but it matters here because repression events (Palmer Raids interrupting the CLP-CP unity conference by ~3 weeks, `www.marxists.org/history/usa/parties/cpusa/1920/0214-clp-questionofunity.pdf`) can freeze a pending mass-line correction — the two mechanics need a coupling contract for what happens to an in-flight SOCIAL_INVESTIGATION/Congress action when a Desert-triggering raid fires mid-resolution.

- **Rank ≠ intelligence.** Multiple cases (Stalin's total silence on German fascism through 1930-31 while mid-level cadre engaged with it, `subject/fascism/blick/ch19.htm`; the KPD's doctrinal blindness to the NSDAP's 1930 breakthrough despite public election data, same source) show I_c does *not* improve monotonically with organizational seniority or centrality. If the engine's Intel Confidence is implemented as a single scalar per faction, this historical pattern is lost; the brief flags but does not resolve whether I_c should be tracked per-cadre-node rather than per-faction.

- **Self-inflicted Desert via paranoia is potentially worse than real infiltration.** The Minsaengdan Incident (Jiandao guerrilla bases, 1932-36): a false-flag informant panic led to a purge of roughly **2,000 cadre against 7-8 actual infiltrators** over ~3 years (`prolewiki/.../Library_Reminscences With the Century_ ... Volume 4.txt`) — a documented ~250:1 false-positive-to-real ratio. This is a genuinely open design question: should the EH mechanic model a distinct "paranoia" sub-state that can be *more* destructive than the Desert condition it responds to, with its own trigger and decay curve? History says yes emphatically; the current spec (M_r/I_c/three vision-states) has no obvious slot for it.

- **Contradictory numbers on repression's suppressive effect.** Some sources show repression suppressing M_r sharply and immediately (Nat Turner's six-week post-rebellion concealment, `prolewiki/.../Library_Nat Turner's Slave Rebellion.txt`); others show diminishing returns over decades (`archive/lenin/works/1902/feb/15.htm`, "the population is gradually becoming inured... ceasing to react to fresh injections") and cases where mobilization continued growing *through* a major raid (IWW AWO, 1917, `25years.htm`). No single repression-decay function fits all cases; recommend the coefficient be scenario-scoped (acute-crisis decay vs. long-run repression-saturation) rather than a single global constant, and flagged for playtest tuning per scenario type rather than one universal default.

- **No source gives a raw baseline M_r growth-per-tick number** for ordinary (non-threshold-crossing) AGITATE_LOCALLY/SOCIAL_INVESTIGATION activity — every quantified growth case in the corpus is a threshold/phase-change event. The steady-state background rate (section 2.1's 0.01-0.02/tick figure) is *not* historically derived and must be treated as a playtest-only placeholder, explicitly distinct from the threshold-jump multiplier which does have historical grounding.
